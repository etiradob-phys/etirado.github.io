About me

Hi there! I am Eduardo Tirado, a Mexican physicist with a genuine interest in Heliophysics with a master's degree from the Instituto Nacional de Astrofísica Óptica y Electrónica.

My main research interest stems from Cosmic Ray Physics. I am also interested in the physical processes behind the interplanetary and near-Earth environment. These include scientific computing applied to heliophysics. My background is in Physics. In 2017 I graduated from Benemérita Universidad Autónoma de Puebla. In 2018, I enrolled in a master's program in Space Science. In October 2019, I spent a one-month traineeship in Belgium at the Royal Observatory of Belgium, and, from December 2019 to February 2020, I realized a three-month internship in Brazil at the Instituto Nacional de Pesquisas Espaciais.

As of January 2023 I am PhD student at the Instituto Nacional de Astrofísica Óptica y Electrónica in the Space and Interplanetary Medium group under the supervision of Dr. Eduardo Mendoza Torres.
